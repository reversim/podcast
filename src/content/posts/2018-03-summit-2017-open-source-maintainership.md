Eternal glorious fame aside, what do the life of an open source maintainer truly look like? This talk will expose some often overlooked aspects of being a maintainer, from what drives sleep away, through the Chinese Exposure and down to the Rise of the Machines. And while this talk is titled "musing and ranting" it will be mostly about ranting. And the Rise of the Machines.</div>
