Starting from the algorithms which are at Outbrain's core, and ending with Internal and Customer reporting, the Outbrain backend is a data processing monster. As the company grows, our data processing needs grow as well, leading to very complex dependencies between the various processes. These dependencies form a growing challange, both from an operational viewpoint and from a development viewpoint. The Outbrain River infrastructure has been created in order provide a solution for this challenge.<br>
<br>
Outbrain River provides the following major features:<br>
<br>
<br>
<ul style="text-align: left;">
<li>Declarative job definitions</li>
<li>Event-driven dependency management</li>
<li>Decentralized development of data flows</li>
<li>Ops-level managability</li>
<li>Out-of-the-box support for JDBC and Hive/Hadoop, easily extensible to any other unit-of-work</li>
</ul>
