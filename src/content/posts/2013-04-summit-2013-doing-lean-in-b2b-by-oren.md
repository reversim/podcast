I will share my experience in following lean principles in B2B startups, to overcome some of the main challenges B2B startups face: finding target customers, getting quality feedback and scoping minimal feature sets for iterative-validation.<br>
<br>
The talk will go over my personal experiences as well as things I've seen effective in many of the Totango clients I work closely with.<br>
