I equally love computer engineering and stand-up comedy. So in this lecture I'm first going give a brief overview of stand-up comedy (in America): history, types and what are the traits of the great stand-up comedians. Following this overview I'm going to show 3 stand-up comedians who used to be engineers and how to managed to connect to the "regular" audience. The presentation will include about 20 minutes of videos (in English), the talk is in Hebrew.<br>
