Swift is a shiny new language for iOS development, introduced during WWDC 2014 (June).<br>
We'll try to answer the following questions about Swift:<br>
<br>
<ul style="text-align: left;">
<li>Is Swift production ready?</li>
<li>What is the current state of Swift and its development tools?</li>
<li>Should you learn Swift or Objective-C if you have no background in iOS development?</li>
<li>Should you write your next iOS app in swift if you already know Objective-C?</li>
<li>I have an existing app with an existing Objective-C codebase. Should I:</li>
<li>Port it entirely to Swift?</li>
<li>Write new classes in Swift and keep maintaining Objective-C code as is?</li>
<li>Keep developing it in Objective-C?</li>
<li>What are some general pros &amp; cons of Objective-C vs. Swift?</li>
<li>And more...</li>
</ul>
