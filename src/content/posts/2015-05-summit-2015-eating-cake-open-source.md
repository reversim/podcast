Most companies can't (or won't) open source their code, maybe for good reasons. In this talk I will show how you can open source some of your code, but leave your core business closed source. I will show how I did it in Kaltura with a closed source application and will demonstrate the advantages for this method - which include both technical and cultural advantages.<br>
