Coderetreat is a day-long, intensive practice event, focusing on the fundamentals of software development and design. It is also a lot of fun.<br>
We at Kenshoo are doing those periodically - hear why, and learn how you can start as well.The newcomer into this world is overwhelmed with information, patterns, tools and practices. Having delivered numerous such projects, I'll try to separate the wheat from the chaff.<br>
This is a clear recipe for the key practices you should keep when building continuously delivered microservice.</div>
