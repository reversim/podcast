<span style="background-color: white;">Scala is a multi-paradigm functional/object-oriented programming language. As such, its design and feature promotes more robust designs, building on immutable value objects and collections, a shared-nothing parallelism model and generally much less lines of code. All of these, in addition to powerful support for in-language DSLs, allow us to grow our software to be testable and deterministic, and is an excellent choice for projects using the TDD methodology.</span></div>
