Meekan developed apps for iOS and Android, and an Outlook Add-in, and threw them all away to build a chatbot. It was apparently a good move: The scheduling assistant is used in over 10,000 companies worldwide.<br>
What happened in the world of chatbots in the last year? What made them so popular? Is it just hype, or is there more to it?<br>
We'll go over the benefits of bots over apps, how to make them more appealing than a command-line tool, and how to adjust yourself to the new Conversational UI.</div>
