Yes, there IS such a thing as the Israeli games industry!<br>
As a kid, I dreamt of developing computer games. This dream came true almost by incidence when I stumbled upon my current job, and realized there's a bustling community of Israeli game developers.<br>
I will share with my impressions of this flourishing local industry as a newcomer, and offer a few tips for anyone who wishes to make games for a living.<br>
