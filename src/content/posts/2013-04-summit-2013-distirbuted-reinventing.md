A company without offices? Employees from all around the world? Working without pants? This surely must be The Future, and Automattic - the company behind WordPress.com - has been living it since 2005. Join me to learn how more than 130 employees from over 24 countries collaborate to make the web a better place. This talk will cover communication tools and methods, hiring and onboarding process, continuous deployment, and the general awesomeness of the distributed company.<br>
