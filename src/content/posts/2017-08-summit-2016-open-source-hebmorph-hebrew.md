Search engine technologies (Lucene, Solr, Elasticsearch) have poor support for Hebrew out of the box. מה לעשות, עברית קשה שפה. The only way around this was to create a custom Lucene analyzer that is smart enough to disambiguate Hebrew words and manipulate the index accordingly.<br>
Today HebMorph is being used by many users, some under an OSS license and some under a commercial license to support the future of the project</div>
