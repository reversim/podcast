Have you ever developed an open-source project? Are you the only user? Been There... In this talk, I will share lessons learned while transforming an open source project from a one man show to a collaborative effort.<br>
We will discuss the journey, "marketing" channels, and ways to make your "product" accessible and consumable. As time allows I will also introduce Kotlin programming language and kotlin-logging lib that I maintain.<br>
A similar talk was submitted to Reversim 2016, but one year and 200 stars later I have more info to share.</div>
