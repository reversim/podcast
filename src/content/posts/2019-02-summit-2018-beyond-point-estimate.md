Recommender systems need to optimize a delicate balance between exploring new recommendations and exploiting informative ones. Up until this point recommender systems employed mostly bayesian prediction algorithms and utilized the inherent measures of uncertainty to optimize the crucial exploration/exploitation balance. While DNNs obtain state of the art results, they present specific challenges for recommender systems due to their lack of standardized uncertainty measures needed for most exploration strategies.
